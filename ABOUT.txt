Welcome to Snowman!

Snowman is a word-guessing game for one or two players. Guess the word or your snowman will melt!

Features

1 or 2 Player mode
In 2 Player Mode, Player 1 enters a word for Player 2 to guess. If you're stuck in your house due to the most recent COVID lockdown, or just don't have any friends, select 1 Player Mode and the game will select a word for you!

Have a Guess
Player 2 (or the friendless Player in 1 Player Mode) has 7 chances to guess a letter in the word or the whole word. With every incorrect guess, the snowman melts a little more... don't let him turn into a puddle!

WINNING
If the word is correctly guessed, players will be transported to a winter wonderland!

Play Again?
Whether the game is won or lost, who could resist playing again? Players get the chance to start the game again or exit the program.
